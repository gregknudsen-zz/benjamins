Gift Cards

Benjamin’s offers gift cards in any denomination and they can be used for all items served in our restaurant. We accept Visa, MasterCard, Discover and American Express. You may purchase a gift card personally at our restaurant or call us and we will be happy to accommodate you by mailing one to that special person.
