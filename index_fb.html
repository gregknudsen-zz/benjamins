Welcome


Welcome to Benjamin’s Restaurant, where we are
always celebrating life, good health and the love
of food! Serving Indiana, since 1996, Benjamin’s
was designed to be a very unique restaurant, mixing casual dining outside, with a vintage chick atmosphere inside. Whether you choose to dine outside or in, we offer a rich ambiance, superb food & outstanding personal service.

Inside, explore all seven dining rooms of the Victorian house, decorated in what we like to call “Vintage Chic’. Relax & dine surrounded by memorabilia of days past. Outdoor dining at Benjamin’s is filled with guests enjoying fabulous drinks and spirits, great conversation and delicious food of which we use only the freshest ingredients. Patio heater allow year round enjoyment so guests can savor dinner under the old grape arbor, enjoy drinks & great conversation around the fire pit or play a game on our bocce court. Either way, you’ll be me with caring hospitality, comfortable surroundings & exceptional value.
